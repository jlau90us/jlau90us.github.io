Early Life
Justin Lau is the Digital Transformation Leader for GE Aviation in Evendale, OH. While there, he works on developing internal and external Digital Solutions for GE's military jet engines business.

Prior to joining GE, Justin served as a pilot in the US Air Force where he flew the E-3 AWACS (airborne warning and control system) where he accumulated over 1700 flight hours in two deployments to the Middle East.

He has a Bachelors of Science in electrical engineering from the US Air Force Academy, a Masters of Science in Engineering from Purdue, and is working towards his MBA from The Kellogg School of Management.
